About this app What is this app?

Welcome to our user-friendly calorie-tracking app! The "Today" card gives you an overview of daily calories and nutrients. Head to the "Find" tab to effortlessly log your meals. On the "BMR" card, calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate for personalized calorie goals. Stay mindful and enjoy the journey to a healthier you!
